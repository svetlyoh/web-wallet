# POLYGON PLAZA PANIC Art Spec

This game is original and inspired by late-90s arcade toy-diorama energy (not copied assets).

## Style Pillars
- Isometric miniature mall/plaza diorama.
- Chunky glossy low-poly forms.
- Bright primaries, teal/magenta/yellow accents, arcade bloom.
- Readable silhouettes first, rich micro-detail second.

## Character Spec
- **Arcade Tech Ace**: rounded helmet silhouette, cyan/yellow palette, utility strap, badge pin, cable clip, shoe stripe, readable face at small size.
- **Mall Safety Cadet**: boxy shoulder silhouette, soft blue/red palette, reflective strips, lanyard, belt pouch, radio stub.
- **Neon Skater**: slim dynamic silhouette, magenta/aqua palette, knee pads, patch jacket, shoelaces, bracelet details.

## Enemy Spec
- **Capsule Drone**: clear acrylic shell, chrome joints, barcode sticker, LED eye.
- **Receipt Wraith**: curled paper-ribbon silhouette, faded print lines, torn edges, tape patches.
- **Service Bot**: rounded plastic/chrome body, warning decal, vent slits, screw heads.
- **Sticker Mine**: flat vinyl puck, starburst label, peel marks, flashing ring.

## Prop Spec
- **Directory Kiosk**: backlit map panel, "YOU ARE HERE" dot, smudges, screws, dented edge.
- **Arcade Cabinet**: marquee glow, side art panel, worn button deck, coin slot scratches.
- **Prize Machine**: curved transparent shell, chrome claw arm, prize clutter, warning decal.
- **Payphone Terminal**: cream shell, worn keypad, coiled cable, tiny blinking status light.
- **Food/Kiosk Props**: trays, cup lids, crumbs, sauce packets, warm menu glow.
- **Toy Displays**: blister plastic, cardboard backing, price sticker, scuffed corners.
- **Electronics Stack**: CRT bezels, blue/green indicator lights, cable clutter, spec cards.

## Boss Spec
- **Prize Core Cycler**: multi-part transparent shell + rotating core, bright weak-point nodes.
- **Neon Serpent Sign**: segmented tube-like body, pulsing signage materials.
- **Escalator Colossus**: ribbed tread armor, amber warning lamps, polished edge wear.

## Readability Rules
- High contrast enemy/projectile colors against floor.
- Top highlights + dark side faces for depth clarity.
- Pickups glow and pulse; hazards use warm danger hues.
- Secrets signaled by subtle blink patterns, not clutter.
